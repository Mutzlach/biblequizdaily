Why Take a Bible Prophets Quiz?

The prophets of the Old Testament were God's messengers who spoke His words to His people. Their messages of warning, hope, judgment, and restoration are still relevant today. Understanding the prophets helps us understand God's character and His plan for humanity.

Our Bible Prophets quiz covers both major prophets (Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel) and minor prophets (Hosea through Malachi). Test your knowledge of their prophecies, their historical contexts, and the messages they delivered to Israel and Judah.

What You'll Learn:

  • Key prophecies from major and minor prophets
  • Historical context of prophetic ministry
  • Messianic prophecies pointing to Jesus
  • Messages of judgment and restoration
  • The role of prophets in Israel's history
  • Prophetic visions and symbolic actions

Whether you're studying the prophets for personal growth, preparing to teach, or just curious about these powerful messengers of God, this quiz will challenge and inform you. Each question includes the biblical reference so you can dive deeper into God's Word!
