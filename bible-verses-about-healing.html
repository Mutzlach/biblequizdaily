What the Bible Really Says About Healing

One of His covenant names is Yahweh Rophe — the LORD who heals.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Bible say about healing? ▼
James 5:14-15 gives the church's instruction: Is any among you sick? Let him call for the elders of the assembly, and let them pray over him, anointing him with oil in the name of the Lord. Isaiah 53:5 speaks prophetically: by his wounds we are healed.
Does God heal today according to the Bible? ▼
The New Testament records healing as a gift of the Spirit and a sign of the kingdom. James 5:14-15 gives instruction for healing prayer to the church without time limitation. Hebrews 13:8 says Jesus Christ is the same yesterday, today, and forever.
What is the difference between physical and spiritual healing in the Bible? ▼
The Greek word sozo is used for both salvation and physical healing. Isaiah 53:5 speaks of both spiritual and physical restoration. While God does heal physical bodies, the New Testament prioritises spiritual healing - the forgiveness of sin - as the greater miracle.
