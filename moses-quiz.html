Test Your Knowledge of Moses - The Great Deliverer

Moses is one of the most important figures in the Bible‚Äîa prophet, leader, and lawgiver who led Israel out of Egyptian slavery. Our Moses Bible quiz covers his incredible journey from baby in a basket to giving the Law on Mount Sinai.

Why Study Moses' Life?

Moses' story teaches us about God's faithfulness, deliverance, and how God uses ordinary people for extraordinary purposes. From the burning bush to the parting of the Red Sea, Moses' life demonstrates God's power and presence.

Major Events in Moses' Life:

  • Birth and Rescue: Hidden in a basket, adopted by Pharaoh's daughter
  • The Burning Bush: God's call to deliver Israel from Egypt
  • The Ten Plagues: Water to blood, frogs, locusts, death of firstborn
  • Passover: The blood on the doorposts that saved Israel
  • Red Sea Crossing: God parts the sea for Israel's escape
  • Wilderness Provision: Manna, water from rock, quail
  • Mount Sinai: Receiving the Ten Commandments
  • Golden Calf: Israel's rebellion and Moses' intercession
