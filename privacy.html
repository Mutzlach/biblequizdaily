Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2024

At Bible Quiz Daily ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website biblequizdaily.com (the "Site").

Key Points:

  • You can use Bible Quiz Daily without creating an account
  • We collect minimal personal information
  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • You have control over your data

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you optionally create an account, we may collect:

  • Name: Your first name or display name
  • Email Address: For account management and communications
  • Quiz Progress: Your scores, completed quizzes, and streaks

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, quiz performance
  • Local Storage Data: Quiz progress stored in your browser
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors use our Site
  • Advertising Cookies: To display relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 To Provide and Maintain Our Service

  • Deliver quiz content and track your progress
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Provide personalized quiz recommendations
  • Enable leaderboard and competition features

2.2 To Improve Our Service

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Develop new features and categories
  • Fix technical issues and bugs
  • Optimize performance and loading times

2.3 To Communicate With You

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send updates about new quiz categories (if you opt in)
  • Provide important service announcements

2.4 To Display Advertisements

  • Show relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Measure advertising effectiveness
  • Fund our free service to keep it accessible to all

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our Site or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3.2 Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our Site. Google Analytics collects information anonymously and reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. Learn more about Google Analytics privacy here.

3.3 Content Delivery Networks (CDNs)

We use CDNs to deliver content efficiently. These services may process your IP address and location data to optimize content delivery.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our Site (e.g., hosting providers, analytics services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition of our business

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

If you use our Site without creating an account, your quiz progress is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
