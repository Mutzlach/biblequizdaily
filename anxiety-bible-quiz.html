Find Biblical Comfort for Your Anxious Heart

Anxiety affects millions of Christians today, but God's Word offers powerful promises of peace and comfort. Our Anxiety Bible quiz helps you discover and memorize the Scriptures that speak directly to worry, fear, and finding rest in Christ.

What the Bible Says About Anxiety

Scripture doesn't ignore our struggles with anxiety‚Äîinstead, it offers divine comfort and practical wisdom. From Philippians 4:6-7 commanding us to "not be anxious about anything" to 1 Peter 5:7 telling us to "cast all our anxiety on Him," God's Word provides a prescription for peace.

Key Bible Verses for Anxiety Covered:

  • Philippians 4:6-7 - Don't be anxious, but pray with thanksgiving
  • Matthew 6:25-34 - Don't worry about tomorrow
  • 1 Peter 5:7 - Cast all your anxiety on Him
  • Psalm 23 - The Lord is my Shepherd
  • Isaiah 26:3 - Perfect peace for steadfast minds
  • 2 Timothy 1:7 - God gave us power, not fear
  • John 14:27 - Peace I leave with you
  • Jeremiah 29:11 - Plans to give you hope and a future
