What Does the Bible Say About…?

What does the Bible say about anxiety? +

The Bible addresses anxiety directly in Philippians 4:6-7: "In nothing be anxious, but in everything, by prayer and petition with thanksgiving, let your requests be made known to God. And the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts and your thoughts in Christ Jesus." Other key verses include 1 Peter 5:7, Matthew 6:34, and Isaiah 41:10. Select "Anxiety & Worry" above to see all 21 curated verses.

What does the Bible say about strength? +

The Bible teaches that true strength comes from God, not human effort. Philippians 4:13 says: "I can do all things through Christ, who strengthens me." Isaiah 40:29 adds: "He gives power to the weak. He increases the strength of him who has no might." And 2 Corinthians 12:9 reveals: "My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness." Select "Strength" above to see all 21 verses.

What does the Bible say about hope? +

Biblical hope is not a wish — it is a confident expectation. Jeremiah 29:11: "For I know the thoughts that I think toward you, says Yahweh, thoughts of peace, and not of evil, to give you hope and a future." Romans 15:13 adds: "Now may the God of hope fill you with all joy and peace in believing, that you may abound in hope, in the power of the Holy Spirit." Select "Hope" above to see all 22 verses.

What does the Bible say about victory? +

Victory in Scripture belongs to God and is shared with believers through Christ. 1 Corinthians 15:57: "But thanks be to God, who gives us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ!" Romans 8:37: "No, in all these things, we are more than conquerors through him who loved us." Select "Victory" above to see all 21 verses on spiritual victory.

What does the Bible say about fear? +

God addresses fear more than almost any other subject — the command "do not be afraid" appears over 365 times. 2 Timothy 1:7: "For God didn't give us a spirit of fear, but of power, love, and self-control." Isaiah 41:10: "Don't you be afraid, for I am with you. Don't be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you." Select "Fear" above to see all 22 verses.

What does the Bible say about forgiveness? +

Forgiveness is central to the gospel — God forgives freely and calls us to forgive others. 1 John 1:9: "If we confess our sins, he is faithful and righteous to forgive us the sins, and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness." Psalm 103:12: "As far as the east is from the west, so far has he removed our transgressions from us." Select "Forgiveness" above to see all 22 verses.
