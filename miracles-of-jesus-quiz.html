Test Your Knowledge of Jesus's Miracles

The miracles of Jesus demonstrate both His divine power and His compassion for humanity. Our Miracles of Jesus quiz covers the supernatural signs and wonders that authenticated His ministry and revealed His identity as the Son of God.

Why Study Jesus's Miracles?

Jesus performed miracles to prove His divine authority, show compassion to the suffering, and point people to faith in God. From healing miracles to nature miracles to raising the dead, each miracle reveals something profound about Christ's character and mission.

Types of Miracles You'll Encounter:

  • Healing Miracles: Blind see, deaf hear, paralyzed walk, lepers cleansed
  • Nature Miracles: Walking on water, calming storms, feeding thousands
  • Exorcisms: Casting out demons and evil spirits
  • Resurrections: Raising Jairus's daughter, widow's son, and Lazarus

Perfect For:

  • Sunday School: Teach children about Jesus's power and compassion
  • Bible Study Groups: Deep dive into the purpose and meaning of miracles
  • New Believers: Learn foundational stories of Jesus's ministry
  • Faith Building: Strengthen trust in God's power through these accounts
